Common Interview Questions

2019-08-29

I will list the frequently asked questions and give answers recommended by Youtubers.

1. Tell me Something about yourself

  • Interpretation: To see your confidence, To see how you express yourself and how is your body language.

  • Hinnts:

    • Name: My name is Yang Xueya(Don’t say it if mentioned)
    • Place: I come from Mainland China.
    • Highest Qualification: I have recently graduated from University of Hong Kong.
    • Skills and Experience:
    • Goals：
    • Ending: That is all about me. Thanks for giving me the wonderful chance.

  • Sample Answer: Thank you very much for giving this oppotunity to introduce myself. My name is Yang Xueya. I belong to Mainland China. I am a recent computer science post graduate from University of Hong Kong. When I was in the College, I had made up my mind to become a software engineer. I have studied the courses of programming, database and project management. I also applied what I have learn in a few projects. Currently, I want to settle in a good organization where I can get a good platform to be a professional software developer. I have heard about XX for years. I always admire XX’s execellence for your professional services and insights. I believe XX would be the perfect enviorment for my future career. Therefore, I want to be a part of XX

    That is all about me. Thanks for giving me the wonderful chance to introduce myself.

2. Why do you want to work in my company?

  • Interpretation: What is important to you about this company? Will you leave for a better offer? Say something praise to the interviewer.
  • Prepare: Research the company before attend the interview. Know something about the past, present and future of the company.
  • Sample Answer: I have always wanted to work for excellent corporate because I always admired what they do. Google is a great company which encourage creation and innovation. This could promoted me after I worked in this company. Therefore, it is always my dread job.

3. What is your greatest accomplishment?

  • Interpretation: 1. Recent accomplishment 2. STAR: Situation, Task, Action, Result. 3. About the career. 4. What is the benefit you get from.
  • Sample Answer: I was the topper among my classmates during my undergraduate and my university awarded me scholarship multiple times for my good performance. And this also brings me to University of Hong Kong, where I get further education with excellent professors and students. That’s my biggest achievement of my life till now but I believe there are more to come in my near future.

4. Are you a team player?

  • Interpretation: Add a strong example to show that you are really a team player. Add some skills which a team player show. Ex: flexible, good listener
  • Sample Answer: Yes I am very much a team player. In fact, there are many opportunities during my school to build up my skills as a team player. For example, in my recent project, I worked with a lady to finish our graduation project. We are complementary and we learned a lot from each other. When meeting conflicts, we will do some survey to backup our opinion and the one with stronger proof will persuade the other. Finally, we finished the project on time and with good result
